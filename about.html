Our Story

Window Worx was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: provide the Des Moines metro with the highest quality window cleaning service, delivered by people who genuinely care.

What started as a small operation in Waukee has grown into a trusted residential window cleaning company serving homeowners across Waukee, West Des Moines, Clive, Urbandale, and the greater Des Moines metro. Through it all, we've remained a family-owned and operated business with two dedicated operators who take pride in every job.

We believe that clean windows transform a home. They let in more light, improve your view, enhance curb appeal, and simply make your home feel better. That's why we treat every home like it's our own.

"We're not happy until you're thrilled with your sparkling clean windows."

More Than a Business — A Commitment to Our Community

Window Worx isn't just about clean windows — it's about being a part of the Waukee community. Since 2009, we've built our reputation one home at a time, earning the trust of neighbors across the Des Moines metro.

As a family-owned company, we understand the value of a well-maintained home. Clean windows don't just brighten your living space — they protect your investment, improve energy efficiency, and make your home feel more inviting. That's why we treat every property with the same level of care we'd give our own.

We're proud to be a local business serving local families. From our first job in 2009 to hundreds of satisfied customers today, our focus remains the same: deliver exceptional quality, show up on time, and treat every customer like family.
