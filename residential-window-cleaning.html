Expert Home Window Cleaning You Can Trust

At Window Worx, we've been helping homeowners in Waukee, West Des Moines, Clive, Urbandale, and across the Des Moines metro enjoy crystal-clear views since 2009. Our residential window cleaning service is thorough, professional, and tailored to your home's specific needs.

What's Included in Our Residential Service:

  • Exterior Window Washing — All exterior glass surfaces cleaned with professional-grade tools and purified water for a streak-free shine
  • Interior Window Cleaning — Inside glass surfaces cleaned carefully, including hard-to-reach panes
  • Track & Sill Cleaning — Removal of dirt, debris, and buildup from window tracks and sills
  • Frame Wiping — Cleaning of window frames to remove dust and grime
  • Screen Cleaning — Gentle washing of window screens (ask about our screen service)
  • Walk-Through Satisfaction Check — We review the work with you to ensure every window meets our standards

Our Residential Cleaning Process

We use a proven approach that delivers consistent, high-quality results:

  1. Assessment — We evaluate your windows, note any problem areas, and discuss your priorities
  2. Preparation — We protect your landscaping, floors, and furnishings
  3. Cleaning — Using professional squeegees, scrubbers, and safe cleaning solutions
  4. Detail Work — Tracks, sills, frames, and screens receive special attention
  5. Final Inspection — We walk through with you to ensure your complete satisfaction

How Often Should You Clean Your Windows?

Most homeowners benefit from professional window cleaning 2-3 times per year. Spring and fall are the most popular seasons. However, homes in certain conditions — near busy roads, new construction areas, or with many trees — may need more frequent cleaning.
