Professional Commercial Window Cleaning

Window Worx provides professional mid-rise commercial window cleaning for buildings in Des Moines and the surrounding metro. Whether you manage an office building, medical complex, or multi-story commercial property, we have the equipment and experience to handle windows up to 4-5 stories high.

Our Commercial Service Includes:

  • Exterior window cleaning for buildings up to 4-5 stories
  • Annual service contracts available — full clean once per year
  • Professional-grade water-fed pole system for upper windows
  • Streak-free results every time
  • Fully insured and bonded for commercial work
  • Flexible scheduling to minimize disruption to your tenants or business

Annual Contract Model

We specialize in annual service contracts where we provide a complete window cleaning every summer. This is a popular model for commercial properties that want consistent, professional results without the hassle of managing multiple vendors.
