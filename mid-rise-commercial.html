Professional Home Window Cleaning

Your home's windows are one of the first things people notice. Keep them looking their best with professional residential window cleaning from Window Worx. We've been serving homeowners across Waukee and the Des Moines metro since 2009, and we treat every home like our own.

What's Included in Our Residential Service:

  • Exterior window glass cleaning — streak-free every time
  • Window frames, sills, and tracks cleaned and wiped down
  • Screen removal, cleaning, and reinstallation (add-on available)
  • Removal of cobwebs, dirt, pollen, and hard water spots
  • Professional-grade tools and purified water for a spotless finish

Our Approach

We don't cut corners. Each job gets our full attention, from the first window to the last. Our two-person team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your day, and we always do a final walkthrough to make sure you're happy with the results.

Why Regular Window Cleaning Matters

Beyond the obvious curb appeal, regular window cleaning helps extend the life of your windows. Buildup from hard water, pollen, and environmental debris can degrade seals and frames over time. A professional cleaning every 6-12 months keeps your windows in top condition.
