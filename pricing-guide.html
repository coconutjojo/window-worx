Our Pricing Philosophy

At Window Worx, we believe in fair, transparent pricing. Every home is different, so we provide free, no-obligation estimates tailored to your specific needs. Below is a general guide to help you understand what goes into pricing and what you can expect.

What Affects the Cost

  • Number of windows — The more windows, the more time required
  • Number of stories — Multi-story homes require additional equipment and safety measures
  • Interior vs. exterior only — Interior & exterior cleaning costs more than exterior-only
  • Window condition — Heavily soiled windows with hard water spots may require extra treatment
  • Screen cleaning — Adding screen cleaning is an affordable upgrade
  • Accessibility — Hard-to-reach windows may require additional time and equipment

Typical Price Ranges

Based on our experience serving homes across the Des Moines metro, here are typical price ranges for common home sizes:
