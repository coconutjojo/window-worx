Bring Your Screens Back to Life

Over time, window screens collect dust, pollen, cobwebs, and grime that block the view and reduce airflow. At Window Worx, we carefully clean each screen — removing them, washing them gently, and reinstalling them — so your windows look better and your home breathes easier.

Our Screen Cleaning Service Includes:

  • Removal of all window screens
  • Gentle but effective cleaning to remove dust, pollen, and grime
  • Inspection for tears, holes, or damage
  • Careful reinstallation
  • Screen repair for minor tears and holes

Screen Repair

If we find any damage during cleaning, we'll let you know and can repair minor tears and holes on the spot. For more significant damage, we can discuss replacement options. Our goal is to leave your screens looking and functioning like new.

Why Clean Your Screens?

Clean screens don't just look better — they actually let more fresh air into your home. Dust and pollen buildup can block airflow significantly. Regular screen cleaning also extends the life of your screens by preventing buildup that can lead to deterioration.

🔲 A Note on Screen Maintenance

We recommend cleaning your screens at least once a year — ideally in the spring before you start opening windows for the season. Combined with our window cleaning service, it's a quick and affordable add-on that makes a big difference.
