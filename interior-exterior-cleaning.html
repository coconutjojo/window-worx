The Complete Inside + Outside Clean

At Window Worx, we believe in doing the job right — inside and out. Our comprehensive service covers every surface, so you don't have to lift a finger. Whether you need just the exterior, just the interior, or both (most popular), we've got you covered.

Exterior Window Cleaning

Our exterior service uses professional-grade equipment and techniques to remove dirt, pollen, hard water spots, and grime from your home's windows. We clean:

  • All exterior glass surfaces
  • Window frames and trim
  • Storm windows and screens (with screen service)
  • Removal of cobwebs and insect debris
  • Hard water spot treatment

Interior Window Cleaning

Our interior service is meticulous and careful. We take pride in leaving your indoor spaces spotless:

  • Interior glass surfaces, both sides
  • Window tracks and sills — dirt and debris removed
  • Window frames wiped down
  • Careful attention to hard-to-reach windows
  • No mess — we protect your floors and furnishings

The Window Worx Difference

Unlike DIY washing or basic services, we take a comprehensive approach. Our two-person team works efficiently to clean both interior and exterior in a single visit. We use purified water and professional squeegee techniques for a streak-free finish that lasts.

💡 Pro Tip

Cleaning both interior and exterior at the same time is the most cost-effective approach. Our combo service saves you time and money compared to scheduling separate interior and exterior visits. Plus, you get the full "wow" factor of perfectly clean windows from every angle.
