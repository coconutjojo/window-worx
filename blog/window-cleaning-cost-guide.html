Window Cleaning Cost Guide — How Much Should You Pay in Iowa?

A detailed breakdown of pricing for professional window cleaning in the Des Moines metro.

Home/ Blog/ Window Cleaning Cost Guide

Published: February 1, 2025 | By Window Worx

If you're searching for "window cleaning cost," you probably want a straight answer. Here it is:

For most homes in Waukee, West Des Moines, Clive, and the Des Moines metro, professional window cleaning costs between $100 and $450+ depending on your home size and services needed.

But there's more to it than a simple number. Let's break down what determines the price, what you're actually paying for, and how to make sure you're getting a fair deal.

Average Pricing by Home Size

Most window cleaning companies in Central Iowa price by the number of windows and the difficulty of access. Here's a general guide based on typical homes:

  • Small home (ranch, 1-story, ~10-15 windows): $100–$175
  • Average home (2-story, ~16-25 windows): $175–$300
  • Large home (2-story+, ~26-35 windows): $300–$450
  • Extra-large / estate homes (36+ windows): $400+

These ranges typically include both interior and exterior cleaning of all standard windows, plus screens if applicable. Always ask what's included in the quoted price.

What Affects the Price?

Beyond just the number of windows, several factors can impact your final cost:

  • Window size and style: Large picture windows, bay windows, and specialty shapes take more time and skill
  • Access difficulty: Second-story windows, hard-to-reach skylights, windows above landscaping or tight spaces
  • Condition of windows: Heavily soiled windows with hard water stains or years of buildup require more labor
  • Interior vs. exterior only: Exterior-only cleans are typically less expensive than full interior + exterior
  • Screen cleaning: Most companies include screen cleaning in the price, but not all — confirm before booking
  • Track and sill cleaning: Some packages include detailed track cleaning; others treat it as an add-on

What Should You Look for in a Quote?

When comparing window cleaning prices, don't just look at the bottom line. Consider what's included:

  • ✅ Are both interior and exterior panes included?
  • ✅ Are screens removed, cleaned, and reinstalled?
  • ✅ Is the company insured and bonded?
  • ✅ Do they use professional tools (not just rags and spray bottles)?
  • ✅ Is there a satisfaction guarantee?
  • ✅ Do they clean window tracks and sills?

Is Professional Window Cleaning Worth the Cost?

Short answer: Absolutely. Here's why:

  • Results you can't get with DIY: Professional tools and techniques produce streak-free, spotless results every time
  • Time savings: What takes you an entire weekend takes a pro crew 1-2 hours
  • Window protection: We use proper techniques that won't scratch or damage your glass
  • Inspection included: We spot window issues early and notify you — saving you money on repairs
  • Curb appeal: Clean windows dramatically improve your home's appearance from the street

When you break it down, professional window cleaning works out to roughly $5–$12 per window for a full interior + exterior clean. That's a small price for windows that look amazing and last longer.

How Often Should You Budget for Cleaning?

Most homeowners in Central Iowa schedule professional cleaning twice a year — once in spring and once in fall. That means your annual window cleaning cost is typically $200–$600 per year depending on home size.

Some companies offer discounts for recurring service or booking both cleanings at once — worth asking about!
