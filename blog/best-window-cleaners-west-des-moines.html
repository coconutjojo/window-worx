Best Window Cleaners in West Des Moines — What to Look For

How to choose a window cleaning company you can trust with your home.

Home/ Blog/ Best Window Cleaners in West Des Moines

Published: February 1, 2025 | By Window Worx

West Des Moines homeowners have options when it comes to window cleaning. A quick Google search shows multiple companies vying for your business. So how do you choose the right one?

As a family-owned company serving the Des Moines metro since 2009, we've seen a lot of window cleaning businesses come and go. Here's our honest advice on what separates the best window cleaners from the rest — and why it matters for your home.

1. Insurance and Bonding — Non-Negotiable

This is the single most important factor. Window cleaning involves ladders, heights, and working around your home. If a cleaner gets injured on your property or accidentally damages something, you need to be protected.

Always ask: Are you insured and bonded?

A reputable company will have general liability insurance and workers' compensation. At Window Worx, we're fully insured and bonded — so you have peace of mind every time we clean your windows.

2. Experience and Track Record

Window cleaning looks simple, but doing it well — without streaks, scratches, or damage — takes skill and experience. Companies that have been in business for years have refined their techniques and built a reputation they need to protect.

We've been serving West Des Moines and the metro since 2009. That's over 15 years of learning, improving, and earning our customers' trust. We've also earned Angie's List Super Service Award (2020) — an honor only given to the top-rated service providers.

3. Professional Equipment and Techniques

There's a big difference between someone with a bucket and a squeegee from the hardware store and a professional window cleaning company. Look for:

  • Professional-grade squeegees and tools — not mops and rags
  • Deionized water systems — for spot-free drying on exterior windows
  • Proper ladder safety equipment — stabilizers, standoffs, and experienced ladder use
  • Uniformed, trained crews — not random day laborers

4. Transparent Pricing

The best window cleaners give you a clear, upfront price — not a "per window" estimate that balloons once they start counting. Look for companies that offer transparent pricing with clear descriptions of what's included.

At Window Worx, we provide free, no-obligation estimates with everything spelled out. No hidden fees, no surprises — just honest pricing for quality work.

5. Satisfaction Guarantee

A company that stands behind its work will offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, they make it right — simple as that. We don't consider a job complete until our customers are 100% satisfied.

6. Local Reputation

Online reviews are helpful, but local reputation matters even more. Ask your neighbors, check community Facebook groups, and look for companies with deep roots in the community.
